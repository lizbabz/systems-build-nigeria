[02] The Practice Ifako · Lagos · Nigeria

Three decades of concrete, steel,
and careful decisions.

Systems-Build (Nig.) Limited was incorporated in December 1994 as a design-build and construction management firm serving the building, civil engineering, and oil & gas sectors across Nigeria. The practice has grown steadily — not explosively — because the work has always asked for judgement first, and speed second.

From the head office in Ifako, Lagos, the team has delivered factories, residential estates, bank branches, warehouses, bridges, fencing at scale, and full refurbishments — taking projects from site survey to handover, and managing the trades in between. Nationwide capability. One standard of finish.
